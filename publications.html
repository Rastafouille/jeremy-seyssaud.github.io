Peer-Reviewed Publications

  • OV2SLAM on EuRoC MAV Datasets: A Study of Corner Detector Performance
    IEEE International Conference on Imaging Systems and Techniques (IST), 2023

    This paper evaluates the impact of different corner detectors on the performance of the OV2SLAM visual SLAM framework using the EuRoC MAV datasets. The study highlights how detector choice and tuning directly affect localization accuracy and robustness in visual-inertial navigation scenarios.

International Conferences & Posters

  • A Multi-Criteria Navigation Framework for Robotic Missions in Complex Environments
    QCAV 2025 – International Conference on Quality Control by Artificial Vision

    Presentation of a multi-criteria navigation framework designed for robotic missions in complex and constrained environments, combining safety, efficiency and mission-specific constraints.

  • Indoor UAV for Nuclear Investigation: A Feedback
    DEM 2024 – International Conference on Decommissioning Challenges

    Feedback on the deployment of indoor UAVs for nuclear facility investigation, focusing on navigation constraints, sensing payloads, operational safety and real-world limitations observed during experiments.

  • Development of a 360° Video Application for Hostile Environment Investigations
    DEM 2024 – International Conference on Decommissioning Challenges

    Description of a 360° video acquisition and visualization system aimed at improving situational awareness during robotic investigations in hazardous and radioactive environments.

  • Agile and Intuitive Robotics for D&D Operations: Use Case of Automated Laser Cutting with Waste Sorting
    DEM 2024 – International Conference on Decommissioning Challenges

    Presentation of an agile robotic solution combining laser cutting and waste sorting for dismantling operations, emphasizing usability, robustness and integration into industrial workflows.

  • Casimir: System-Level Testing for Electronics Equipment in D&D Operations
    DEM 2024 – International Conference on Decommissioning Challenges

    Overview of system-level testing methodologies applied to electronic equipment used in dismantling and decommissioning operations, with a focus on reliability and qualification.

  • Assessment of Ultrasonic Cutting Technology for Nuclear Dismantling Projects
    DEM Conference, 2021

    Evaluation of ultrasonic cutting technologies for nuclear dismantling, including performance, limitations and integration constraints in radioactive environments.

  • Remote Operated Aspiration Device for Waste Lying on the Floor of Casemates
    DEM 2018 – Avignon, France

    Design and deployment feedback of a remotely operated aspiration system dedicated to waste retrieval in confined and radioactive casemates.

  • Gamma Imaging Investigation of a Nuclear Casemate
    ATSR 2018 – Codolet, France (Poster)

    Poster presenting feedback on gamma imaging techniques applied to nuclear casemate investigation using robotic platforms.

  • Robot Operating System and Nuclear Dismantling
    2RM Robotics Day, 2017 – Lille, France

    Conference on the use of ROS as a middleware for robotic systems dedicated to nuclear dismantling applications.

  • SERVAL Project: Tele-Operation of a Robotic Arm for Nuclear Dismantling
    Innorobo, 2017 – Lyon, France

    Presentation of a tele-operated robotic arm developed for nuclear dismantling tasks, focusing on control architecture and operator interaction.

  • Robotic D&D: Smart Robots
    WMS Journal Conference, 2015 – Phoenix, Arizona, USA

    Overview of smart robotic systems for decontamination and dismantling operations, addressing autonomy and human-robot interaction.

  • Investigation Robot on Cables
    WMS Journal Conference, 2015 – Phoenix, Arizona, USA

    Presentation of a robotic system designed for inspection tasks along cables in constrained industrial environments.
